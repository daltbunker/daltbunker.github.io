Personal Interests

Just over 10 years ago I stumbled upon rock climbing. From that year on I would spend my week days in the climbing gym, weekends, summers, or any other chance I had out on the rocks. Climbing is my life, I've traveled around the world from Europe to South Africa. My favorite places to climb include but are not limited to Moab, Wyoming, Tennessee, and France.

Evey now and then I enjoy a day at the skate park. Skateboarding is something I grew up with but never fully invested myself in. Perhaps it's because the concrete isn't very soft. Regardless I still love it and find it a nice excuse to play in the sun. I like to cruise around in the bowls and play on mini ramps. The newer skatepark in Lehi is probably my favorite spot.

One other interest of mine is videography. I starting dabbling in this field a few years back. I enjoy the creative process of video editing. As you might guess my film subject is mostly rock climbing.
